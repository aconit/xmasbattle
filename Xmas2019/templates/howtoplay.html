How to play

In this game you need to solve the ultimate logistics problem Santa faces every year: load the sleigh with as many gifts as possible for one ride.

You compete against Santa Qlaus - the quantum version of Santa. But don't worry. His quantum algorithm is not perfect yet, so you have enough chances to win the battle.

The game consists of 5 rounds. Each round starts with some information:

- The amount of gifts shows from how many gifts you'll have to choose.

- Max loading is the maximum weight your sleigh can carry in this round. If the sum of weights of the chosen gifts exceeds the max loading weight, you lose the round.

- Time shows the amount of seconds you have to make your choice. When the time is up you won't be able to change your choice.

Choose gifts by simply checking the checkbox. The label of the chosen gift turns white.

The user (you or Santa Qlaus) with the highest sum of weights of the chosen gifts, but less than the max loading weight of the sleigh, wins the round. The game is over after 5 rounds.

Enjoy!
